**STND-04 -- Pit stop count:**

- Scrub past the first pit stop window (~lap 15-20) -- confirm pit count increments to 1 for drivers who pitted
- Scrub to end of race -- confirm pit counts match expected (typically 1-2 stops)

**Position changes:**
- Scrub between two consecutive laps where a position change occurs -- confirm green up arrow and red down arrow appear on the correct drivers
- Confirm arrows disappear when positions are stable

**Visual quality:**
- Confirm team color bar on left edge of each row
- Confirm driver abbreviation shows full name tooltip on hover
- Confirm ~10 rows visible without scrolling, remaining drivers accessible by scrolling
- Confirm dark theme consistency with the rest of the dashboard
